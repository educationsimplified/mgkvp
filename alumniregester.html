What is Alumni Network and Support?

An Alumni Network is a community of former students who have graduated from a university or department. This network serves as a powerful resource for professional development, mentorship, and staying connected with your alma mater. Alumni Support refers to the various services and benefits offered to registered alumni, such as career guidance, networking events, access to job opportunities, and continued learning resources. By registering, you become part of this valuable ecosystem, helping both current students and fellow alumni thrive.
